Semantic HTML

Tag Name Description
<Header> Title of Page The header is a semantic tag where your title and usually your navbar maybe located.
<Main> Main The main objective or collection of the page.
<Article> Article of the Page The article or blog a section where it is described.
<Aside> Aside of the Page The aside is when you want to add information that maybe related but not in the main.
<Nav> Navigation of the Page The place where the navigation of the page is encountered.
